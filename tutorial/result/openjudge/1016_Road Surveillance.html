1016:Road Surveillance

  • 查看
  • 提交
  • 统计
  • 提问
总时间限制:
2000ms
内存限制:
65536kB
描述

Driving over speed is dangerous. It is common practice to set up surveillance cameras along a road to capture any speed violations. To ensure full coverage, the police department is going to upgrade the surveillance system on the road. First, new cameras will be installed at strategic locations. Second, a new model of camera with a new video range will be applied. However, the longer the video range, the more expensive the camera is. Therefore, the police department want to choose the new type of camera with its video range minimized.


The road to monitor is a straight road. We use the distance to the start point of the road to denote a location on the road. Therefore, the road starts from zero and ends at some given point e. There already exist n cameras along the road that will be upgraded to the new models. But their locations cannot be changed. The police department will install m cameras at some new locations. Each camera at location p will cover the road segment [p, p+r], where r is the video range of the camera. Note that one of the n existing cameras is known to be at point 0.


输入
The first line is the number of cases T.
For each test case, there will be three integers on the first line, e, n and m. 1<=e<=10^9, 1<=n<=10^5, 0<=m<=10^5.
On the second line, there will be n-1 integers, representing the locations of the existing cameras except the one at point 0. The locations will be given in increasing order.
输出
For each test case, determine the minimum possible video range required of the new cameras to surveil the entire road ([0, e]). Output the results to the third decimal point.
样例输入
2
4 4 1
1 2 3
10 3 2
5 6
样例输出
1.000
2.500
