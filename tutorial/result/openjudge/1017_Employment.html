1017:Employment

  • 查看
  • 提交
  • 统计
  • 提问
总时间限制:
1000ms
内存限制:
65536kB
描述

Company KOOL has a human resource department for hiring employees. To effectively select qualified employees, the human resource department maintains a database of applicants. Each applicant is given a unique id based on their application order. The first applicant gets 1, the second gets 2, etc. As soon as the applicant applies, the department will review the applicant's resume and analyze his/her capability, resulting in a scaled integer score in [0, 100]. When necessary, the department will interview the applicant later, probably for several rounds, and update the capability score based on the last round performance. Sometimes applicants might withdraw their applications so that they will not be further considered. When another working department needs a new employee, the requesting department will announce a score requirement, and the human resource department will hire the qualified and most capable (with highest score) applicant in the current database, as long as his/her capability meets the requirement. When two applicants have a same score, the one that applies first get employed. You are invited by the department to decide whom to hire.

输入
The input has several lines (not exceeding 10^5 lines), each is one of:
1) a letter 'a' and an integer s: an applicant applies with the initial capability score s assigned based on the resume;
2) a letter 'i', an integer k and an integer s: an interview updates the score of the applicant whose id is k to s;
3) a letter 'w' and an integer k: the applicant whose id is k withdraws;
4) a letter 'h' and an integer s: hire a new employee who has the highest capability score no less than s.
There will be no more events about an employee who has been hired or has withdrawn.
输出
For each hiring event, output the hired employee's application id, as well as his/her capability score at the moment, on a single line. When no qualified employee is available, output a single zero on that line. You do not need to output anything for the other events.
样例输入
a 80
a 70
a 50
a 70
h 75
a 90
a 80
i 6 85
i 5 60
h 50
h 90
w 5
h 70
样例输出
1 80
6 85
0
2 70
