1003:Cannot Be Fungi

  • 查看
  • 提交
  • 统计
  • 提问
总时间限制:
1000ms
内存限制:
65536kB
描述

Fungi has very divergent thinking. When he speaks to someone, he switches his topics from time to time. Sometimes a current topic may be interrupted by a new inserting topic. But Fungi's thinking pattern is very rigid. He always requires the first started topic to be finished last, that is, each time when Fungi finishes one topic, he switches to the previous topic that has not been finished yet. Fungi will never let this order go wrong. Therefore, to verfiy whether Fungi is in a discussion, simply check the sequence of the finished topics. Try that now!

输入
The first line of the input contains the number of cases, T (T<=10^3).
T cases follow. At the beginning of each case will be an integer k (k<=10^3) indicating the number of finished topics, followed by k space separated integers representing the ids of the topics. You may assume that with k topics, the topics ids will go from 1 to k. There will be no repeated topic ids in one case.
输出
For each case, output "May be Fungi" if the sequence matches Fungi's talking style. Otherwise, output "Cannot be Fungi!" (exclamatory mark included).
样例输入
3
5 5 4 3 2 1
3 3 1 2
4 3 4 2 1
样例输出
May be Fungi
Cannot be Fungi!
May be Fungi
提示
For the last sample case, the topics are discussed in this order: topic 1 started, topic 2 started, topic 3 started, topic 3 finished, topic 4 started, topic 4 finished, topic 2 finished, topic 1 finished.
