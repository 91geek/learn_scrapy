描述

The office for the anniversary of Peking University has decided to use a digital display to show some meaningful information. The display has identical digits, and each digit has line segments. Each segment can be either on or off. With these segments, a digit can represent all ten digits and some capital letters including "P", "K" and "U".  You can see from the following figure what a digit looks like.

At time , the inital state of the display is "2017PKU119", which is shown below.

During each time step, the display will automatically perform one of the following operations:
1. Turn on a segment which was originally off.
2. Turn off a segment which was originally on.
3. Replace a lightened segment, i.e. turn on a segment which was originally off while turn off a segment which was originally on.
4. Turn on two segments which were originally off.
5. Turn off two segments which were originally on.

All operations are considered as different transitions between two states of the display. The office wants the display to show "2018PKU120" for the first time at time , where is a positive integer. In other words, the display should not become the desired state at some time , but should show "2018PKU120" at time . The desired state is shown below.

A question then arises: how many different ways of transitions which satisfy the requirement of the office are there? You may only tell the office the answer modulo .

Two ways of transitions are considered the same if and only if they let the display show exactly the same pattern at every time t .
