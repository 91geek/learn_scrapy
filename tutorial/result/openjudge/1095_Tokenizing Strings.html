1095:Tokenizing Strings

  • 查看
  • 提交
  • 统计
  • 提问
总时间限制:
1000ms
内存限制:
65536kB
描述

String tokenization is to split some paragraphs into words, delimited by space, dot(.), comma(,), question mark(?), exclamation mark(!) or newline.

Your task is to output the tokenized strings from some given paragraphs. Your tokenized strings shall appear in the order as they are in the original paragraphs. Also your tokenized strings shall not contain the delimiting characters. Note that if two delimiters are consecutive, you shall ignore the empty string between them.

输入
There is only one test case, which is some paragraphs containing letters, spaces or punctuation. Note that only the characters mentioned above are delimiters.

Each line of the input will contain at most 200 characters (not including newline).
输出
Tokenize the paragraphs and output the tokenized strings. One string on each line.
样例输入
The game takes place in Liberl Kingdom, 10 years after the great invasion of Erebonian Empire from the north. 

Currently it is being governed by the old queen Alicia II and restored back to its peaceful state before the war. Currently 
the kingdom thrives of materials and high technology which is eyed closely by the Erebonian Empire.
