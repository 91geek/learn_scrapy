C15J:Stars in Your Window II

  • 查看
  • 提交
  • 统计
  • 提问
总时间限制:
1000ms
内存限制:
65536kB
描述

Do you know the problem Stars in Your Window in POJ Contest 2005? That problem is famous for its description, which is an incredibly poetic love letter and tells a romantic story but with a mournful ending.

We do not know if the boy has shown his love after graduation, but we hope that the lovers are finally coupled up.

Now let us consider another scenario. Ten years later, the girl finds this love letter on POJ while she is teaching her son programming. She is touched by the last paragraph -- “If someday, somewhere, we have a chance to gather, even as gray-haired man and woman, at that time, I hope we can be good friends to share this memory proudly to relight the youthful and joyful emotions. If this chance never comes, I wish I were the stars in the sky and twinkling in your window, to bless you far away, as friends, to accompany you every night, sharing the sweet dreams or going through the nightmares together.”

She looks at the sky and wants to find the stars recording the old but vivid memory.

We assume that the sky is a plane and there are N stars in the sky. The coordinate of the i-th star is (Xi, Yi). The girl wants to calculate the number of axis-aligned rectangles whose four vertices are all stars. Note that there are no two stars lying at the same coordinate.

输入
There are several test cases.

For each test case, the first line contains an integer N, indicates the number of stars. 1 ≤ N ≤ 100000.
For the next N lines, the i-th line contains two integers Xi and Yi indicate the coordinate of the i-th stars. 0 ≤ |Xi|, |Yi| ≤ 1000000000.
输出
For each test case, output one line with an integer, indicates the number of axis-aligned rectangles.
样例输入
6
0 0
0 1
1 1
1 0
0 2
1 2
16
1 1
1 2
1 3
2 2
2 3
3 1
3 3
3 4
4 -1
4 1
4 2
4 3
4 4
5 1
5 2
5 4
样例输出
3
14
