描述

A roomcontains nbirdcages, each right next to each other. Each birdcage has a bird in it, andlocked.
One night, the man who is the owner of the room, decides to play a game.For round 1 of the game, he takes a drink of whiskey, and then runs down theroom unlocking each birdcage. For round 2, he takes a drink of whiskey, andthen runs down the
room locking every other birdcage (birdcage s 2, 4, 6, ?). For round 3, hetakes a drink of whiskey, and then runs down the room. He visits every thirdbirdcage (birdcage s 3, 6, 9, ?). If the birdcage is locked, he unlocks it; ifit is unlocked, he locks it. He
repeats this for n rounds, takes a final drink, and passes out.
Some number of birds, possibly zero, realizes that their birdcages are unlockedand the man is incapacitated. They immediately escape.
Given the number of birdcages n(0<=n<=1000,000, determine how many birdsescape.


输入
Input the number of birds,one a line,to the end of file.
输出
Output the number of escaped birds.
样例输入
0
2
10
样例输出
0
1
3
