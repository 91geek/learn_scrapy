1030:Stock Market

  • 查看
  • 提交
  • 统计
  • 提问
总时间限制:
1000ms
内存限制:
65536kB
描述

Stock market is dangerous, but also quite fun, posing a big challenge on your investment mind. Fortunately, with the help of a  strong consultant, you can predict the trend of stock market with high success rate. However, even if you foresee the trend of the market, it is not easy to make right decisions.


Suppose you have S bucks initially. Your consultant has predicted the trend of the market for the next n time points. At each time point, you can buy any amount of stocks if affordable, or sell any amount of stocks you have. One stock will cost you pi bucks to buy and also sell for the same price at time ti. What is the largest amount of money you can have by the end of tn?


输入
The first line has an integer T, the number of test cases (T<=100).
Each test case has two integers, n and S on the first line (1<=n<=100, 1<=S<=10^6). The next line has n integers, the i-th representing pi. 1<=pi<=1000.
输出
For each case, output the maximum money you can have by the end of tn.
样例输入
1
3 10
4 1 3
样例输出
30
提示
Stock market is really dangerous.
