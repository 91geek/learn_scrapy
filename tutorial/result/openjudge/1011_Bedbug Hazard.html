1011:Bedbug Hazard

  • 查看
  • 提交
  • 统计
  • 提问
总时间限制:
1000ms
内存限制:
65536kB
描述

Bedbugs are annoying insects that feed on human blood. They are only active at night and go without sleeping man's notice. They do three bites at a time and cause skin rashes. They are hard to be caught and hard to kill. Crafty, disgusting, mentally haunting as they are... Please! No more bedbugs at home!

It is known that a bedbug can move a very large distance to get on its prey. Now a bedbug warrior has coincidently dropped by somebody's home. You, the bedbug expert, is invited to carry out safety investigation in the potential victim's house.


输入
The first line is the number of cases T.
Each case has three integers, n, m and d, on the first line (1<=n,m<=200). d is the maximum distance a bedbug can travel to get feed. Then follows a map as n×m characters in the next n lines, in which:
1)'#' denotes a wall that the bedbug cannot cross;
2)'.' denotes empty space that the bedbug can move into;
3)'*' denotes the drop-by location of the tiny bedbug warrior;
4)'v' denotes the victim's position in the house.
The bedbug can move in four directions: up, down, left or right. The distances between nearby blocks are all one. There will be exactly one bedbug warrior and one victim in the map.
输出
For each case, if the victim is not within the bedbug's reach, output "peaceful night". If the victim is within the bedbug's reach, you have to find out a minimum distance the victim can move in order to avoid being bitten by the bedbug (move to a block that is d' away from the bedbug warrior, d'>d). Then output the movement distance Δd as "Δd steps to peaceful night" (use "steps" even when Δd=1). Note that the victim is allowed to move through bedbug reachable grids to his final destination. If no such escape is possible, output "nightmare".
样例输入
3
8 6 10
......
...###
...#*.
......
#.####
...#.v
...#..
......
5 8 6
........
.##..##.
.#v..*#.
.##..##.
........
6 5 9
.....
..#..
.*#..
.###.
..#v.
.....
样例输出
peaceful night
6 steps to peaceful night
nightmare
