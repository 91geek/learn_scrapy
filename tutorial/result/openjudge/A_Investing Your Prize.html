A:Investing Your Prize

  • 查看
  • 提交
  • 统计
  • 提问
总时间限制:
1000ms
内存限制:
262144kB
描述

As a lucky guy, you have just won the first prize of P Yuan in the lottery!

Since you are not satisfied with the low interest rate of bank savings, and are also afraid of losing money in Chinese Stock Market, you finally decide to use the prize to purchase an investment product.

Now you are interested in investment products provided by Surpass Quantitative Technology company, the sponsor of our PKU Campus 2016. For the i-th investment product, the money you paid for purchasing should be at least liYuan, and the expected annual rate is ri%. In another word, if you spend M Yuan for purchasing the i-th investment product, then M should be not smaller than li, and you will get M·(1+ri%) Yuan after a year.

So, you are wondering that, which investment product is the best one to invest the prize in, in order to maximize your money a year after?

To simplify the problem, you are only allowed to use all your prize to purchase only one of the investment products for the entire year, and the expected annual rate of each product is always accurate.

输入
The first line contains an integer T (1 ≤ T ≤ 10), indicating the number of test cases.

For each test case:

The first line contains two integers N (1 ≤ N ≤ 10) and P (100,000 ≤ P ≤ 100,000,000), indicating that there are N different investment products, and you have got a prize of P Yuan in the lottery;

Then follows N lines, the i-th line contains an integer li (1,000,000 ≤ li ≤ 100,000,000) and a real number ri (1 ≤ ri ≤ 50, reserves exactly two digits after the decimal point), indicating that the i-th investment product requires at least li Yuan for purchasing, and the expected annual interest rate is ri%.
输出
For each test case, output one integer x on a single line, indicating that purchasing the x-th investment product is the best choice. It is guaranteed that there is one and only one best choice.
样例输入
1
3 10000000
10000000 3.51
20000000 7.36
5000000 2.99
样例输出
1
