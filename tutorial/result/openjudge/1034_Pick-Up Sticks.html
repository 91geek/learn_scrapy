1034:Pick-Up Sticks

  • 查看
  • 提交
  • 统计
  • 提问
总时间限制:
1000ms
内存限制:
65536kB
描述

Pick-up sticks is a popular games among children. In the game, you scatter a bunch of sticks onto the desk and try to remove one stick at a time so that the other sticks don't move. This is very challenging as the interrelations between sticks are complex. The safest way to remove a stick is to remove one that is not below any other stick. The other ways may also work, but posing a big risk. Fungi often plays this game when he was young. Fungi does not want to take any risk of losing the game, so he wants to remove sticks only in the safest way. Also, he wants to remove as many sticks as possible. Help him find such a way.

输入
The first line has an integer T, the number of test cases (T<=60).
Each test case has two integer n and m on the first line (1<=n<=1000, 0<=m<=10000), denoting there are n sticks in total and m below relations. Then follow m lines, each contains two integers a and b, indicating stick a is below stick b. Any two sticks will not have a mutual below relation.
输出
For each case, output an integer k first, indicating the maximum number of sticks Fungi can remove in the safest way. Then output k integers on the same line, which is the sequence of removed sticks. When there are multiple ways to safely remove the maximum number of sticks, output the lexicographically smallest sequence.
样例输入
1
5 3
2 1
3 1
4 1
样例输出
5 1 2 3 4 5
提示
For the sample input, stick 5 does not touch any other stick. However, as stick 1 can also be removed safely, to get the lexicographically smallest removal sequence, we remove stick 1 before stick 5. After that stick 2, 3, 4 are freed and shall be removed ahead of stick 5 for the same reason.
