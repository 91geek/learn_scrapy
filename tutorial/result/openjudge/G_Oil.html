描述

输入
The first line of input contains a single integer n (1 ≤ n ≤ 2 000), which is the number of oil deposits. This is followed by n lines, each describing a single deposit. These lines contain three integers x0, x1, and y giving the deposit’s position as the line segment with endpoints (x0, y) and (x1, y). These numbers satisfy |x0|, |x1| ≤ 10^6 and 1 ≤ y ≤ 10^6. No two deposits will intersect, not even at a point.
输出
Display the maximum amount of oil that can be extracted by a single oil well.
样例输入
5
100 180 20
30 60 30
70 110 40
10 40 50
0 80 70
样例输出
200
Sample Input #2
3
50 60 10
-42 -42 20
25 0 10
Sample Output #2
25
