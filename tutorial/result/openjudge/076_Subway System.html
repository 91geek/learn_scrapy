076:Subway System

  • 查看
  • 提交
  • 统计
  • 提问
总时间限制:
1000ms
内存限制:
65536kB
描述

The great New York City has an extremely complex subway system that often confuses its passengers. Even with the subway map, a passenger may still have difficulties in figuring out which is the best route to destination. Therefore route planning applications on mobile phones are very popular among subway passengers.

When a user opens the application, the application automatically determines a set of the subway stations close to the user based on GPS. The user may choose to walk to any of these stations to take the subway. After the user chooses a destination station, the application finds the shortest subway routes (in terms of travel time) from each of these station to the selected destination station.

Now company KOOL is developing such a subway route planning application. The GPS location module has already been completed. They need your help in the route planning module.

输入
The first line has an integer T, the number of test cases (T<=20).
Each test case has two integers n and m on the first line (2<=n<=10^5, 1<=m<=10^5), the number of stations and the number of tracks of the subway system. The stations are numbered from 1 to n. m lines follow, each has three integers a,b,t denoting a bidirectional track between station a and b that takes t minutes to travel (a!=b, 0<=t<=10^4). There may be multiple tracks connecting two stations.
After the above there will be an integer k specifying the number of stations returned by the GPS module (1<=k<=n). The next k lines has two integers each. The first is the station number and the second is the time (in minutes, no greater than 10^4) the user needs to walk to that station. Finally there will be an integer as the destination station number.
输出
For each case, output k integers on a single line. The i-th is the minimum time required to go to the destination station if the user chooses to walk to the i-th station returned by the GPS module. It is guaranteed that the subway system is connected.
样例输入
1
7 8
1 2 3
2 3 2
3 4 5
3 5 2
4 5 7
4 7 2
5 6 4
2 6 9
2
1 5
7 2
6
样例输出
16 15
