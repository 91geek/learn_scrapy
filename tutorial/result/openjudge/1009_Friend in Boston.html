1009:Friend in Boston

  • 查看
  • 提交
  • 统计
  • 提问
总时间限制:
1000ms
内存限制:
65536kB
描述

Juno has a friend in Boston. She often goes to Boston to see her friend. The schedule arrangement for the Boston trips are always troublesome and Juno needs to make sure that the Boston trips do not interfere with her daily study affairs at school. In fact, Juno has drawn herself a time table in which there are n possible trips. The ith trip starts from bi(inclusive) and ends at ei(exclusive). Juno wants to see her friend as many times as possible but any two of the trips she actually takes shall not overlap. Help Juno check her time table and maximize the number of Boston trips she can take.

输入
The first line is the number of cases T (T<=50).
Each case has an integer n (n<=10^5), the number of trips on the time table.
Then follows a line with 2n integers, the (2i+1)th and (2i+2)th integers are bi and ei for the (i+1)th trip for i = 0, 1, 2...n-1.
All time will be integers in the range [0, 10^9]. It is guaranteed that bi < ei for all i.
输出
For each case, output the maximum number of Boston trips Juno can take.
样例输入
2
3
0 10 5 13 13 27
6
5 10 5 7 11 14 8 10 9 13 11 17
样例输出
2
3
