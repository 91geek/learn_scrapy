1035:Molecular Chain

1000ms

65536kB
描述

Little Box has discovered a new type of molecular chain. Basically, the molecular chain is a chain of atoms on a 2D plane. The atoms are connected by chemical bonds. The bonds separates consecutive atoms by a fixed distance. The bonds are directional, and each pair of consecutive bonds will form an angle from the degree set {60, 120, 180}. The length of a molecular chain is defined by the number of atoms. A chain with n atoms is called an n-chain. Two molecular chains are considered different if they are not isomorphic. That is, we cannot get one from the other through flipping and rotating. Note that two atoms can share a position in a chain. When this happens, we do not distinguish them by depth as the chain lies on 2D plane. However, the following two molecular 5-chains are considered different.





Little Box wants to know how many distinct molecular chains exist for a given length n.

输入
The first line has an integer T, the number of test cases (T<=100).
Each test case is an integer n, the length of the molecular chain (1<=n<=10^6).
输出
For each case, output the number of distinct n-chains modulo 1,000,000,007.
样例输入
2
3
4
样例输出
3
9
