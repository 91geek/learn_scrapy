描述

Given three circles, each of them locates wholly outside any other circle without any intersections, and the centers of these circles are not on a same line. Find another circle that is internally tangent to all of them.

The picture below is the image of the sample. Three smaller circles inside are given by the input and the largest circle outside is the correct answer.


Your answer (a circle whose center is (X, Y) and radius is R) will be judged correct if, for each given circle (whose center is (X', Y') and radius is R') in the input,


输入
The first line contains an integer, indicates the number of test cases, which is no more than 30.

Each test case contains three lines. Each line consists of three integers X, Y and R, describing a circle's center and radius respectively (0 ≤ X, Y ≤ 1000, 1 ≤ R ≤ 1000).
输出
For each test case, output your answer in a single line. An answer should contain three real numbers X, Y and R, indicating a circle whose center is (X, Y) and radius is R.

If no such solution exists, output “no solution” in a single line.
样例输入
1
40 36 8
27 12 4
17 36 5
样例输出
30.32239573 28.13315241 20.47170058
