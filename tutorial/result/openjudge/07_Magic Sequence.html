07:Magic Sequence

  • 查看
  • 提交
  • 统计
  • 提问
总时间限制:
1000ms
内存限制:
65536kB
描述

A sequence of positive integers can be cut into several segments of data. Each segment consists of adjacent elements of the same value. The magic of this sequence is that, each time when a data segment is cut off, the elements before and after the data segment are automatically connected together as neighbors. For example, after cutting out the data segment “7 7” from the sequence “2 8 9 7 7 6 9 4”, the remaining elements form the sequence “2 8 9 6 9 4”.

If you want to cut the sequence into severaldata segments, how many data segments at least can be cut out?

Example: Cut the sequence “2 8 9 7 7 6 9 4” inthe following order as long as it is cut into 6 segments

Cut "7 7" and the rest sequence is "2 8 9 6 9 4"

Cut "6" and the rest sequence is"2 8 9 9 4"

Cut "9 9" and the rest sequence is"2 8 4"

Cut "2" and the rest sequence is"8 4"

Cut "8" and the rest sequence is"4"


输入
The first line is an integer indicating how many datasets are in total.
The input of each case includes two lines: the first line is an integer N, N<=200, indicating the length of the sequence, and the second line is N positive integers.
输出
The output of each test case is one line, which is an integer, indicating the minimum data segments.

The output format should be:
Case n: x

n is the index of test case, x is the answer.
样例输入
2
8
2 8 9 7 7 6 9 4
16
2 8 9 7 7 6 9 4 4 2 8 4 2 7 6 9
样例输出
Case 1: 6
Case 2: 11
