C:Water PiPes

  • 查看
  • 提交
  • 统计
  • 提问
总时间限制:
1000ms
内存限制:
65536kB
描述

A factory need to build a new water pipes which is M meters long to the water factory. Now the water pipe shop has N kinds of water pipes for sale, the total length of each pipe is k meters, the price is P ＄ / m. You can cut these pipes and buy any length. Now could you help me to count how much should the factory spend at least to build the new water pipes? (Ignore the loss of water pipe stitching)

输入
The first line of two integers m, n
From the next line to the next n+1 line, each line of two integers k, pi represent the total length of the each case water pipe is k meters and the unit price p ＄ / meters.
输出
One integer means the least cost of the building price.
样例输入
500 6
100 10
150 7
500 12
100 6
50 1
80 8
样例输出
3580
提示
m,k，p in integer
n≤100
